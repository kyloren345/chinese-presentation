Origins of Buddhism

Buddhism was made by Siddhartha Gautama Buddha AKA The Buddha who was a philosopher who lived in the 6th - 5th Century BC. He was a religious teacher who taught the ways of Buddhism and made everything about it today. There are many different religious beliefs and religious ways of promoting peace.

Spread of Culture

The Buddha was born in what is present-day Nepal and travelled five years to get to India and teach the ways of Buddhism there and teach the Indians to follow the Middle Path. He travelled throughout the north eastern areas of India for decades, while also spreading his teachings to those who were actually interested in the culture.
